Lasagna

Description

This is my grandmothers recipe that has been passed down from 3 generations.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz) package of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 16oz jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pound ricotta cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste
